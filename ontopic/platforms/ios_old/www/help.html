Welcome to OnTopic, a simple, interactive tool for use with children and adults to help with topic maintenance and social communication skill building!

How to use OnTopic

Conversation topics go inside the colored area (the "circle").
  • Tap to add an icon (such as ★) inside the circle each time the student or client makes an on-topic comment.
  • Tap outside the circle when a comment is off-topic.
  • You can easily remove an icon with a second tap, if corrected.

Setting up a board

Topics can be used during one-on-one conversations, or during group activities.
  • Begin by typing in the name of the student or client (multiple names can go in if used in a group), and deciding a conversation topic.
  • There are options for circle and background board colors, and icons.
  • Choose how many on-topic comments are needed to meet that day's goal
  • Tap Let's talk to start talking!

Activity data

If desired, data from the activity can be saved on your device for progress monitoring and future goal development.

You can access the data by clicking on the Menu button, then View History. You can also turn off data recording by tapping on the Record button.
